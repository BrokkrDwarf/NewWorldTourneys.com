AUS Region Dungeon Speedrun Portal

Welcome to the Australia region speedrun portal, this is where you can view all of the past and current records submitted for every Expedition available in New World!

To view the leaderboards you must click on the link coresponding to the Expedition you want to view.

If you want to submit your own speedrun to our website simply go to our submit your speedrun page and follow the instructions to have a shot at being a record holder.
