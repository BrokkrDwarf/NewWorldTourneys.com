Welcome to NewWorldTourneys.com!

We organise 5v5 Weekly Tournaments between some of the best New World PVP players in the world for awesome cash prizes!

Do you want to be part of the fun?

Simply assemble a team of 5 players and email us at newworldtourneys@gmail.com with your discord username, region and server you play on and we will try to run a tourney on your server as soon as we can!

You can check out our previous tournaments that we have organised in the Archives section of the website.

On this website you can submit the fastest times of your Expedition runs in New World, you can see the leaderboards of each expedition sorted by server region on the Dungeon Speedruns page.

To find out how to submit your own speedrun simply go to the Submit Your Speedrun page and follow the instructions.

You can also ask questions, chat, and connect with the community by checking out our forums (not made yet).


Wanna see it live?

Watch the tournament unfold live on our Twitch Channel every saturday from 6pm GMT.
